# 环境准备


1. 创建 maven 项目，添加依赖
```
    <dependencies>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.apache.hadoop</groupId>
            <artifactId>hadoop-common</artifactId>
            <version>2.8.2</version>
        </dependency>

        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.apache.hadoop</groupId>
            <artifactId>hadoop-hdfs-client</artifactId>
            <version>2.8.2</version>
        </dependency>
    </dependencies>
```
2. 将 `core-site.xml` , `hdfs-site.xml` 复制到项目 `resource`目录
